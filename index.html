About Us

At Chav Mayechi, we believe in preserving the authentic flavors of traditional Maharashtrian cuisine. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the taste of homemade food to every household, just like a mother prepares for her family.

Every product we create is made with pure, natural ingredients, without any preservatives or artificial additives. We follow traditional recipes that have been passed down through generations, ensuring that each bite carries the essence of our rich culinary heritage.

आमच्या "चव मायेची" मध्ये, आम्ही पारंपरिक महाराष्ट्रीयन पाककृतीचा सांभाळ करतो. प्रत्येक गोडवा आईच्या हाताचा, प्रेमाने केलेला. शुद्ध, नैसर्गिक साहित्य वापरून, कोणत्याही कृत्रिम रसायनांशिवाय तयार केलेले स्वादिष्ट लाडू.
